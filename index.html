About Me

Hey, I’m Zane – a front-end web developer passionate about creating beautiful, user-friendly experiences. My mission is to have a positive impact in the life of everyone I encounter through everyday interactions. I’m a Christian, a people person, and a tech enthusiast who finds joy in the little details of web design and development. I specialize in front-end UX and UI design, blending clean code with intentional design to build engaging digital experiences.

My journey began in 2019 when I built my first website, inspired by the creative portfolios I saw online. It quickly grew into a passion that has guided my academic and professional path. Since then, I’ve continued to develop my own site as a sandbox for my learning and creativity. Along the way, I’ve also picked up a love for graphic design, often creating visuals for my projects.

I’m currently a Computer Science major at Union University (Class of 2027), concentrating in web development with a minor in business marketing. Right now, my focus is on growing my skills, passing my classes, and taking on freelance opportunities when they come my way. Outside of tech, I work at Chick-fil-A and serve on the production team at Love and Truth Church, where I mix broadcast audio and program lighting. I also love to write inspirational essays and poetry and play the piano.
